Group Formation

DELIVEET was born from a desire to streamline last-mile delivery operations by making them more efficient and less expensive.

After having first-hand experience of the nightmares that come from lost packages and delayed arrival of important parcels, the need to do something about this constant problem became a vision.

From high cost of deliveries, to missing packages and sometimes delays in scheduled delivery, last-mile delivery which is the last stage in the supply chain process of e-commerce for individuals and businesses happens to be the most frustrating of all. Consumer buying behaviors have changed, especially when it comes to online shopping. Today’s consumers expect cheap—or, better yet, free—and fast delivery. So, in order to stay competitive, companies need to find a way to improve efficiency and cut costs in order to fund their logistics.

A big part of this boils down to a company’s last mile delivery process. That means, for a company to survive in this market, they need to find ways to improve efficiency in this area. The concept of DELIVEET is to utilize a network of local couriers and delivery personels, who are familiar with their areas and can provide on-demand fast and cost-effective last-mile deliveries to individuals and businesses who need them.

DELIVEET was created as a portfolio project to showcase my skills and passion for solving problems with technology and to reflect my learning journey in the ALX(Holberton) Software Engineering’s Cohort 1 Blended program. The development process spanned three weeks with positive brainstorming sessions, late-night code hacks and several interations in order to reach an MVP.

The vision of DELIVEET is to offer a new, more efficient, and better way for individuals and businesses to solve their last-mile delivery problems - delivering convenience one package at a time. DELIVEET aims to make life simpler for people who deserve a better way of getting their things delivered.
